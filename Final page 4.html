La Tomatina

It is always celebrated on the same day, the last wednesday of August of each year and consists of a battle where neighbors and tourists throw tomatoes at each other until the intence red colour of the tomatoes invades all the street of the center of Bunol and people ware white clothes

Semana Santa

During Holy Week, familes gather together for special meals, go to church for Easter Vigils and mass, and creat spectacular alfomnras, or rug like displayes made with flowerin the street. There are parades where we reenct jesus  on the cross and revial of Christ and they ware capirote which is a catholic cone shaped pointed hat made out of silky material.
