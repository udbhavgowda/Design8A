The history of spain traditional clothing?

During the Visigothic period (5th-8th century AD), Spanish clothing took on a more Germanic infuence. Men wore tunics and trousers, while woman wore long dresses with distinctive "Kwyhole" neckline. This period also saw the interduction of the mantilla, a traditional Spanish veil worn by woman.
